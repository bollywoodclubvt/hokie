What is Bollywood Club?

Bollywood Club is a vibrant student organization that celebrates the diversity of Indian cinema and its influence on global culture. As a dynamic club, we provide a platform for students from diverse backgrounds to come together and immerse themselves in the rich culture, dance, music, and film that Bollywood encompasses.

Want to join the Bollywood Community?

Bollywood Club is a vibrant and exciting organization that celebrates and explores the rich culture of Indian cinema and entertainment industry. If you are a bollywood buff or completely new to bollywood, we are the place for you! We will hold food stalls, movie nights, and other events to get the immersive feeling of Indian culture! Stay tuned for announcements and looking forward to seeing you all in future events!
